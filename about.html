About business

Hi there,

Welcome to Andrey and Arif's one-stop shop for your fair-trade organic flower and accesoiries supply.

We are here to help you make someone very happy in a fair and sustainable way.

Buying presents with us is like making a donation to the cause of a fair, ecologically clean and sustainable world.

We would love to make sure you leave our shop happy and reassured that the money you spent here goes to the right hands, those of good people like you, who try their best to live and work in a fair and dignified way.

Thanks for doing your gift shopping with us and helping us to keep things fair.

Andrey and Arif
