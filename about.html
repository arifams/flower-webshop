About business

Hi there,

Welcome to Andrey and Arif's one-stop shop for your fair-trade organic flower and accesoiries supply.

We are here to help you make someone very happy in a fair and sustainable way.

Buying presents with us is like making a donation to the cause of a fair, ecologically clean and sustainable world.

We would love to make sure you leave our shop happy and reassured that the money you spent here goes in the right hands, those of good people like you, who try their best to live and work in a fair and dignified way.

Thanks for doing your gift shopping with us and helping us to keep things fair.

Andrey and Arif

About Ourselves

Andrey product, marketing and research

After almost 6 years of managing my own flower shop, I have a failry good knowledge of the 'green' branch. I know where the flowers and plants come from, and how unfair the working conditions often are for the people who grow them.

My background is in environmental studies at Wageningen University (Netherlands), where I graduated from in 2005. This is where I have learned about how importnat international networking is, if you want to create fair working conditions in the product chain.

In the last years the organic segment of our food market has been growing fast. Same growth has been lacking with the flower and plant market. 'Unfair!' did we think and decided to offer only organic products in our shop, and honestly inform you, if that is not the case with one or more products, and why.

What else we do

In our free time, if we have some, we like listening and dancing to electronics music.

We also seriously like nature and animals in particularly, and that not only in our free time.

What we don't like are people who abuse and treat animals cruelly.

This is why, together with our good friends from Honest Electronics we organize alternative music festivals right in the most dangerous for animals places on Earth.

Check out our octomuzz project website to know more about the octo-festival series to prevent slaughtery of sea mammals.
