where the money goes

Oragnic Flowers For Equal Rights (OFFER) is a non-profit based in Jakarta, Indonesia.

A large part of the earning from Ariflora goes to OFFER. With help from our contribution, OFFER has been able to deploy professional education programms for over 4,000 students through in-school, after-school and summer school programms, and approximately 3,000 teens from orphanages and families with under minimal income level.

Your purchase will continue to enrich and strengthen these programms. The funds go directly toward the students, providing them with the support they need to overcome disadvantages and achieve their economic and academic dreams.

sustainable flower farming

80 percent of all flowers sold in the West are imported, primarily from third world industrial flower farms that have a history of using harsh chemical fertilizers, toxic pesticides and unfair labor practices.

With the net of small flower growers we are taking a cue from organic food farmers, who have successfully harnessed the farm-to-table trend. More of us now know where our beef comes from and how our tomatoes are grown, but it rarely occurs to us to ask the same questions about the flowers on our tables.

We sell flowers certified by VeriFlora, signifying growers who meet eco-friendly and fair trade standards.
